Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Thank you for playing Block Puzzle: Puzzle Game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle, collect, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimalism and privacy. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) to play our game.

  • Anonymous Authentication: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to give your device a unique, anonymous ID. This allows us to save your high scores to the global leaderboard without knowing who you are.
  • Game Data: We collect your highest game score and the display name you choose (e.g., "player_1234") to display on the Global Leaderboards.
  • Device Information: Third-party services we use (like Google Analytics for Firebase) may automatically collect basic, non-identifying device information (such as OS version and device model) to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase

3. How We Use Information

The anonymous data collected is used strictly for:

  • Displaying your score and chosen username on the Global Leaderboard.
  • Analyzing game performance and squashing bugs.
  • Improving the overall user experience.

4. Data Deletion

If you wish to reset your game progress and delete your anonymous high score from our system, you can use the "Reset Progress" option located within the in-game Settings menu. If you uninstall the app, your anonymous session is also removed from your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our game is designed for all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
